<i>Wall You</i> is a Wallpaper App built with <a href='https://m3.material.io/'>Material Design 3 (You)</a>. For that matter, the app supports a wide range of wallpaper sources. Most of them are unfortunately nonfree however, thus the anti-feature.

<br><b>Features:</b>

* Wallpaper Browsing and Downloading
* Automatic Wallpaper Changer
* Favorites List
* Wallpaper History
* Image Filter Effects
